# Alert Rules

## Queue Lag High
- Trigger when average wait time exceeds SLA by >20% for 10 minutes.

## Notification Failure Spike
- Trigger when failed notifications exceed 5% of total in 10 minutes.

## Database Saturation
- Trigger when DB connection usage exceeds 80% for 5 minutes.

## Device Offline Surge
- Trigger when >20% of devices are offline within 10 minutes.
